About Us
McEmblem Manufacturing Co.Limited. specializes in manufacturing & exporting metal, soft PVC and lanyard items for business promotion, souvenir and retailing purposes. Our main products include metal lapel pins, keychains, badges, coins, medals, cufflinks, tie bars, dog tags, belt buckles, trolley coins, bookmarks, green repair and soft PVC pins, magnets, coasters, bottle openers, charms, name plates, fridge magnets, luggage tags, lanyards, embroidery patches and more. Other than our open items, we do a lot of OEM projects with customers. At McEmblem customer service always comes first. With over 20 years of experience and expertise in the industry, we always serve our customers all over the world by turning their ideas into products in requested quality, at reasonable price and with quick turnaround. If you are interested in our products or you have own design items for development, please do not hesitate to contact us. We look forward to hearing from you soon.
